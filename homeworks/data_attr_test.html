Instruction

  1. Give data attributes to buttons: data-colour and assign each colours
  2. When you click on buttons, box should change it's background-color
  3. Do NOT give classes to buttons

Optional

  1. Give data attributes to one of the button : data-isCircle="1"
  2. When you click on the button, and the button has data-isCircle=1, then make the box to a circle
  3. Else the button does not have a data-isCircle then make the box to box again.
